MEET OUR TEAM

BOARD

Lucas Ji Chairman Lucas Ji is a Chairman of SYSTRAN Group. He is also a founder of Changshin Soft, and CSLi. With more than 25 years of experience in business management and IT industry, he took over SYSTRAN and grow into a global company specialized in natural language processing and AI.

Denis Gachot CEO Denis Gachot is a CEO of SYSTRAN Software Inc. since 1986 and has experienced decades of SYSTRAN’s impact on technology and culture. He restored SYSTRAN image as a leader developer of machine translation software and lead the company rapid growth in new markets. He is presently responsible for all operational aspects of SYSTRAN Software, Inc., in the Americas. His vision is leading the creation of hyper-ecosystem.

Jean Senellart Global R&D Lead Jean Senellart leads development teams located all around the world. He has been working on the modernization of SYSTRAN’s legacy code, by leading multiple challenges such as Unicode introduction, IntutiveCoding technology development, introduction of machine learning, development of Translation Stylesheet, design and first-ever release of “self-training” hybrid translation engines, and more recently, modularization of language assets into the world-unique “Linguistic Development Toolkit”.

John Paul Barraza Global Business Operating Lead John Paul Barraza leads the company’s operations in the Americas. Globally, he oversees the development of 3rd party ecosystem connectors for SYSTRAN’s industry-leading Enterprise solutions. JP manages cross-functional, global teams working on highly visible and mission-critical projects. With over 14 years of experience in Language Technology and NLP, he is familiar with the unique needs of diverse customers. He has led projects and customer implementations across a wide range of industries.

Dongpil KIM Global Strategic Planning Lead Dongpil Kim is a skilled Chief Operating Officer of SYSTRAN. With more than 22 years experience in IT technology and commercialization Kim has become an expert at driving growth in global business. He is also experienced in partnership, investment, and M&A.

Ken Behan Global Sales& Marketing Lead Ken Behan is responsible for defining and implementing the growth strategy for SYSTRAN. With 20 years’ experience in the Global language industry, he is considered a thought leader in NLP having held several Senior Executive roles within the industry leaders during his career. Ken also brings cross-department and P&L management experience to SYSTRAN through his running of the Dublin office of a US multinational (190 people) for several years. He is a serial entrepreneur as well as a business mentor to several Irish Start-ups.
